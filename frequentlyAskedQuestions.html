Frequently Asked Questions
COVID-19

Will school be in-person for the 2020-2021 school year?

This school year, CHS is operating on a hybrid plan where half of students will attend school Mondays and Tuesdays and the other half will attend Thursday and Friday. Each Wednesday all students and faculty will learn from home while the schoolâ€™s custodial staff cleans and disinfects the building between cohorts.


Can students still buy lunch?

Students will still have the option of bringing their own lunch or ordering through a pay as you go system coordinated through the Wall Township School District.


How will you promote handwashing and general hygiene?

CHS remains deidicated to maintaining a healthy, safe environment for students and faculty. Signage throughout the building, monitoring by teachers and supervising staff, health education in classes, and communication with parents/guardians will maintain this consistent message and reinforce hygiene as a priority while in school.


Academics

How is CHS different from a traditional high school?

Communications High School is a specialized program for students interested in learning more about various fields of communications including TV, radio, design, journalism, and photography.


Is there tutoring available for students who need help in a particular class?

All teachers have time set aside during lunch to assist students who are struggling. Additionally, the National Honor Society offers peer tutoring during lunch.


How much homework do students receive?

With proper time management skills, students receive a very manageable amount of homework each night. Additionally, students have access to open computer labs at lunch if they need to work on an assignment during the school day.


Extracurriculars

What school events are there?

School events help to bring the CHS community together. There are dances and after school events throughout the school year, and the SGA hosts spirit week in the fall and spring. Finally, the PSFA hosts Color Wars every year in June.


Will students need to arrange their own transportation for after school activities?

Yes. Most clubs, however, hold their meetings during the hour long lunch period.


Does CHS have sports teams?

No. Students who wish to participate in sports must return to their home high school.


Transportation

How are students transported to CHS?

Students are transported to school by buses organized by the home district. Seniors with proper documentation are allowed to drive to school, and students may also opt to get a ride from their parents to school.


How are students transported to their home high schools for sports?

Transportation to home high schools for sports is organized with your individual bus company/school district.


Miscellaneous

Will I need to buy a laptop? Should I get a Mac or PC?

It is not necessary to purchase a laptop. All computers at CHS are Macs; however, most programs are compatible with either and many students use PCs as their personal computers.


Do I have to plan on pursuing communications as a career in order to attend CHS?

Not necessarily. Students should express a demonstrated interest in the communications field, but we understand that interests change throughout high school. Many alumni do work in the communications field, but many have also taken up other disciplines.
