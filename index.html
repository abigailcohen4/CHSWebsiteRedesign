Our Tracks

Broadcasting

All students take a Radio and TV broadcasting course at CHS. Our broadcasting courses utilize our recently renovated full radio studio and TV broadcasting space.

Visual Arts

CHS requires two introductory design courses before branching into specified interests, including traditional art, digital art, animation, and photography. Students become proficient in using many Adobe Suite programs.

Digital Video

Students take an introductory course to learn the process of filmmaking, editing, and using camera equipment. Advanced courses further film production skills and promote creativity in the classroom setting.

Journalism

CHS requires an introductory class within the journalism field. Students write and analyze media and pursue this track by taking Advanced Mass Media and/or Public Relations.

Programming

All students take an introductory course to learn the basics of Python. Students can further their coding knowledge by learning Javascript and HTML/CSS in the advanced courses.
